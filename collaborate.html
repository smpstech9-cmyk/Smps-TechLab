How It Works

Our Collaboration Process

A premium partnership journey built for Academia, Startups, Government Agencies, and Industry.

01

Send Your Interest

Share your collaboration interest, project idea, institution requirement, or partnership objective with the SMPS TECH team.

02

Discussion & Requirement Analysis

Our team connects with you to understand goals, expectations, technical needs, timelines, and collaboration opportunities.

03

Mutual Agreement & NDA

Both parties finalize confidentiality, collaboration terms, responsibilities, and partnership understanding through NDA/Mutual Agreement.

04

Proposal & Action Plan

We prepare a customized collaboration roadmap including scope, deliverables, milestones, execution strategy, and action items.
