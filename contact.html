Frequently Asked Questions

What is SMPS Tech Lab and its focus?
SMPS Tech Lab is a National Platform and Innovation Hub located at VTU Regional Center Bengaluru, designed to bridge the gap between academic education and core engineering industry demands.
How can students register for the skill tracks?
Students can enroll in our advanced courses and bootcamps (VLSI, IoT, Embedded Systems) through the Products page or by submitting an inquiry on this Contact page.
How can industry partners collaborate with the hub?
We welcome alliances for talent sourcing, internship programs, and lab setups. Please contact our leadership via +91 90358 74229 or email us at smpstechlab@gmail.com.
