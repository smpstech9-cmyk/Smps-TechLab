Our Purpose

What Drives Everything We Do

Our Vision

To empower every student in India with the practical skills, industry confidence, and real-world experience needed to lead the global technology landscape. We envision a future where "fresher" means "industry-ready."

Our Mission

To provide a high-impact learning environment where students work on live industry projects, mentored by veterans, and graduate as experts. We are committed to closing the skills gap through practical, hands-on engineering leadership.

Our Story

Technology Alone Does Not Define Progress

SMPS Tech Lab was founded with a singular purpose: to solve the biggest challenge facing fresh graduates—the "No Experience" paradox.

As a subsidiary of SMPS Electric, we realized that while the industry was starving for talent, thousands of graduates were struggling to find work. The missing link was practical, industry-standard project experience.

We built this platform to be the bridge. Today, we are a hub where students become professionals by working on actual hardware and software products that serve real-world clients.

Core Operations

Our Operational Mandate & Business Scope

As a legally chartered entity, SMPS Tech Lab is built to drive technological innovation, product engineering, and deeptech commercialization.

Deeptech R&D & Manufacturing

Research, prototyping, system integration, and manufacturing of next-generation deeptech hardware and software solutions suitable for critical sectors including power, railway, defense, space, nuclear, and oil & gas.

Advanced Engineering Systems

Providing specialized engineering, design, testing, validation, and consulting across embedded systems, power electronics, industrial automation, IoT, AI, data acquisition, telecom, and quantum technologies.

Incubation & Innovation Hubs

Establishing and managing innovation-driven platforms, incubation-support systems, proof-of-concept (POC) sandboxes, and applied research environments in physical, digital, and hybrid modes.

IP Development & Licensing

Developing, licensing, and distributing proprietary intellectual property (IP), simulation models, hardware reference designs, software suites, and technical documentation to facilitate tech transfer.

Tri-Sector Collaborations

Partnering with academia, private industry, and government bodies (incubation programs, sponsored research, and pilot deployments) to bridge the commercialization gap for indigenous technologies.

ESDM Technical Skilling

Providing high-impact technical education, training, and ESDM (Electronics System Design & Manufacturing) skilling for new-age engineering students, faculties, and researchers.
